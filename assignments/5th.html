Top and Favorite Movie List

  1. Iron Man
    • The Year it was released: 2008
    • Actor: Robert Downey Jr.
    • After being held captive in an Afghan cave, billionaire engineer Tony Stark creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.
  2. The Avengers
    • The Year it was released: 2012
    • Actor: Robert Downey Jr, Chris Hemsworth
    • Earth's mightiest heroes must come together and learn to fight as a team if they are going to stop the mischievous Loki and his alien army from enslaving humanity.
  3. Avengers: Age of Ultron
    • The Year it was released: 2015
    • Actor: Robert Downey Jr, Chris Hemsworth
    • When Tony Stark and Bruce Banner try to jump-start a dormant peacekeeping program called Ultron, things go horribly wrong and it's up to Earth's mightiest heroes to stop the villainous Ultron from enacting his terrible plan.
  4. Avengers: Infinity War
    • The Year it was released: 2018
    • Actor: Robert Downey Jr, Chris Hemsworth
    • The Avengers and their allies must be willing to sacrifice all in an attempt to defeat the powerful Thanos before his blitz of devastation and ruin puts an end to the universe.
  5. Avengers: Endgame
    • The Year it was released: 2019
    • Actor: Robert Downey Jr, Chris Hemsworth
    • After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.
